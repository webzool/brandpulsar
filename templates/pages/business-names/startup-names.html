What Makes Fantastic Startup Domain Names?

Here are a few factors that affect the visibility and memorability of your startup business name. First, keep it as short as possible to make it easy to remember and type. Choosing a relevant name that communicates a valuable message is also important. If you want to get an exceptional name, do not be afraid of "out-of-the-box" names which are unique and innovative. How to Come Up with Creative Names?

One of the best ways to come up with creative ideas is creating a list of possible names. You should not limit yourself in this stage and write any idea that comes to your mind. You can also check books or dictionaries to find words that have relatable meanings but are unknown to many people. Such startup names can be more meaningful, and it will seem mysterious. Even if you prefer easy-to-understand names, play with them a little. For instance, change the order of the letters or the syllables. Adding different extensions to improve the richness of the startup names is also advisable.

Follow The Standards While Being Creative

Wonder how you can choose the best startup name? First, try not to make it complicated. Short domain names with easy spelling make the website more accessible. Your customers should not memorize whether they should write 5 or five, whether they need to put a dash or not to find your website. Keep it short and straightforward!

Second, choose startup names that represent your business. You can put the brand values or delivered benefits into the name to communicate a reasonable message to the visitors. Even if you like non-existent names, try to understand what feelings the name provokes. A short name can still indicate the ease of the service, and weird names can seem innovative. Let the startup name do the business for you.

Third, make it catchy and relatable. Whether you choose a one-word name or a long one, try to make it as attractive as possible. Your potential customers should be amazed by the name tweaks you make or the message you deliver. Catchy startup names are easy to remember and help your business stand out in the competition where hundreds of similar companies try to survive.

Lastly, test the name. If the startup domain names you like are already taken, make small adjustments. Change extensions, add adjectives, or articles. Get feedback from your potential audience - ask them how they feel toward the name. Plus, use the name in different mediums such as logos or signatures.
