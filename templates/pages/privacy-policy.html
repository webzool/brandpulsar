Cookies

We use cookies to track the activity on our website. Cookies help us to provide users better user experience and improve our service.

Users can always set their browsers to refuse cookies, however this may result for users not being able to use all parts of our website.

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies are stored in the user's personal device and remain when the user goes offline. Session Cookies are session based, which means these cookies help the website remember user actions only for a session and the information is deleted when the user closes the browser.

Cookies we use in our website:

We use both persistent and session cookies for different purposes on our website. We use session cookies to increase the user experience which include providing some features to users. Without cookies, these services cannot be provided. Persistent cookies used for remembering user choices which are essential for being able to use website features. This includes remembering login details and cookie preferences.
