Setup Instructions

Brandpulsar requires that you verify your domain before it can be put on sale. In order to verify your domain, you will need to edit your DNS records and add the TXT record. Don’t worry tho, it’s easy to do and we’ll guide you through the steps:

  1. Sign in to your domain registrar (domain registrar is where you manage your domain; it can be Domain.com, Godaddy, Hover, or any other domain registrar)
  2. Open DNS management page
  3. Next, you should create a TXT record in your DNS with a title of brandpulsar-verification.
  4. The hostname should be @ or {{domain}} for the text record you add.
  5. Save the TXT record.
  6. Login to your Brandpulsar Account >> Dashboard >> Domain Setup Page and Click on Verify.

Note:

Keep in mind that it can take from a few minutes to up to 3 hours to see the saved TXT record
